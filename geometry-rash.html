About Geometry Rash

Geometry Rash is an arcade platformer game that challenges your reflexes and timing with 15 hand-crafted levels of pure geometric mayhem. Keep your eyes wide and your fingers quick! Developed by Vi Games and released on December 4, 2019, this game delivers a thrilling experience that proves simplicity and brutality are a perfect match.

The premise is deceptively straightforward: guide a small geometric square through an auto-scrolling world packed with triangular spikes, moving platforms, and rhythm-timed traps. One mistimed click sets you right back to the beginning — and you will love every restart.

What is Geometry Rash?

Geometry Rash throws you into a neon-colored world where your character — a cute yet fragile square — races forward automatically through a maze of spikes and platforms. Your mission? Keep it alive by helping it jump over deadly triangular obstacles and land safely on platforms.

Simple, right? Not so fast. One wrong move, one mistimed click, and it’s back to the beginning!

How to play

Controls: Just use your mouse — click anywhere on the screen to make your square jump.

Objective: Complete all 15 levels by avoiding spikes and jumping on platforms.

Tips: The game gets trickier as you go. Timing is everything, so keep your focus and don’t let the smooth beats distract you!

Why you'll love it

  • Fast-paced and intuitive gameplay that’s easy to learn, hard to master.
  • Clean 2D visuals with bright, geometric aesthetics.
  • High replay value: Each level is a challenge, and starting over feels more like motivation than frustration.
  • Perfect for casual play but also appealing to competitive players aiming to complete all 15 levels.

Geometry Rash is the perfect game to dive into as you guide the little square through every trap and finish all 15 levels.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Geometry Rash?

Geometry Rash is a rhythm-based runner inspired by Geometry Dash, but with its own original levels, music, and obstacle designs. It is not an official RobTop Games product.

How does Geometry Rash differ from Geometry Dash?

Geometry Rash features independent level designs and music rather than official GD content. The visual style and obstacle types differ, though the jump-to-beat mechanic is similar.

Does Geometry Rash get harder over time?

Yes — obstacle frequency and movement speed increase as you progress through each level, making later sections significantly more demanding than the opening.

Should I play Geometry Rash with sound?

Strongly recommended. All levels are synchronised to their soundtrack and the music cues help you anticipate hazards before they appear visually.
