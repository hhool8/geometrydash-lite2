Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026 · Effective: April 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how GD Lite 2 (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you visit geometrydash-lite2.poki2.online.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information collected automatically

When you visit our site, our servers and third-party analytics tools may automatically collect:

  • IP address (anonymised after 90 days)
  • Browser type, version, and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring URL
  • Device type (desktop, tablet, mobile)
  • Country/region (derived from IP)

This information is used in aggregate form to improve site performance and understand how visitors use our services. We do not sell this data or use it to build individual user profiles.

1.2 Information you provide voluntarily

We do not currently operate a contact form on this site. If you contact us by email, we use your message only to respond to your enquiry and do not pass it to third parties for marketing purposes.

2. Cookies

We and our advertising partners use cookies — small text files stored on your device — for the following purposes:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the site to function correctly.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us count visitors and understand traffic patterns using aggregate, anonymised data (Google Analytics 4).
  • Advertising cookies: Used by Google AdSense to show relevant ads based on your interests.

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling advertising cookies will not remove ads from our site, but ads you see may be less relevant to your interests.

3. Advertising

GD Lite 2 is free to use and supported by display advertising, including ads served by Google AdSense. Ads appear only on surrounding page content — we do not display ads inside active game iframes, and we do not use pop-up, pop-under, or autoplay video advertising.

Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our site or other sites. You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings. Alternatively, opt out via aboutads.info or the NAI opt-out page.

4. Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to measure site usage. GA4 collects anonymised usage data including page views, session duration, and Core Web Vitals performance metrics. Data is retained for 26 months by default. To opt out, install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. Third-Party Game Content

Our pages embed game content hosted on third-party services (such as scratch.mit.edu, game.azgame.io, and others). These third-party iframes may set their own cookies or use local storage; we cannot control this. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any third-party service whose content appears on our pages.

6. Children’s Privacy

GD Lite 2 is intended for users aged 13 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@poki2.online and we will delete it promptly.

7. Data Retention & Security

We retain server log data for up to 90 days. We apply industry-standard security practices including HTTPS encryption. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

If you are in the European Economic Area, the UK, or California, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Erasure: Ask us to delete your personal data.
  • Restriction: Ask us to restrict processing of your data.
  • Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@poki2.online with the subject line “Data Request”.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page reflects when the most recent changes were made. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at privacy@poki2.online.
